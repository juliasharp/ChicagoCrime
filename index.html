Visualizing Crime in Chicago Neighborhoods

Crime in Chicago consistently ranks top 10 in the nation. Much of that is concentrated in a small part of the city. Many of the communities with the highest crime rates, also have the highest concentration of people living in poverty. Fuller Park, fore example, consistently has the highest crime rates at about 1 crime per 20 people, with about half of the community living in poverty.
